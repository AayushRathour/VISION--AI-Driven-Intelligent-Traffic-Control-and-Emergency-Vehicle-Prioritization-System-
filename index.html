How Vision Works

Our intelligent system makes real-time decisions to optimize traffic flow

Emergency Vehicle Detection

Our advanced sensors and AI algorithms instantly recognize approaching emergency vehicles. When detected, the system:

  • Immediately turns lights green in their path
  • Coordinates with adjacent intersections to maintain priority
  • Provides visual and audible alerts to other drivers

Real-time emergency vehicle detection and response

Dynamic traffic light timing based on vehicle count

Adaptive Traffic Flow

Our system continuously monitors vehicle density at each intersection and makes intelligent adjustments:

  • Reduces green light duration when fewer than 25 vehicles are detected
  • Extends green light timing during peak hours or special events
  • Coordinates with neighboring intersections for optimal flow
